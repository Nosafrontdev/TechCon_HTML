History

TechCon began in 2005 as a small local gathering of software developers in Silicon Valley. Initially focused on web development trends, the conference quickly gained popularity for its collaborative workshops and visionary keynote speakers. By 2010, TechCon expanded globally, hosting events in Europe, Asia, and Africa.

Mission

TechCon is driven by a mission to inspire innovation, foster collaboration, and empower the next generation of technology leaders. We believe in open knowledge-sharing, diversity in tech, and creating opportunities for all communities to thrive in the digital age.

Past Speakers

Bisi Salako

Bisi Salako is a renowned AI researcher and the CTO of FutureTech Labs. She has over 15 years of experience in machine learning, robotics, and ethical AI development. At TechCon 2022, she delivered an inspiring keynote on the future of human-centered artificial intelligence.
